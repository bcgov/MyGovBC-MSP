Next Steps

To complete Medical Services Plan enrolment, adult Canadian Citizens and Permanent Residents must obtain a Photo BC Services Card by visiting an Insurance Corporation of BC (ICBC) driver licensing office. To find an ICBC driver licensing office near you, please visit icbc.com.

If you have already been to an ICBC driver licensing office and requested a Photo BC Services Card, no further action is required.

Information

  • Allow 21 business days for your application to be processed.
  • Once your application is processed, you will be sent a letter that confirms your Medical Services Plan enrolment status. You may have a wait period.
  • When you get your BC Services Card, register for the FairPharmacare plan. It can help with the cost of prescription drugs and some medical supplies. Registration is free.
  • Keep your personal information up to date. Change your name, address or gender.
  • Living outside the province for more than six months could cause your Medical Services Plan enrolment to be cancelled. Learn more about eligibility for Medical Services Plan.
  • If you have questions, contact Health Insurance BC.
