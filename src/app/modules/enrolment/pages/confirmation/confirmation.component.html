Next Steps

To complete Medical Services Plan enrolment, adult Canadian Citizens and Permanent Residents must obtain a Photo BC Services Card by visiting an Insurance Corporation of BC (ICBC) driver licensing office. You can now book an appointment at a driver licensing office at a location and time that suits you. For more information, please visit icbc.com.

If you have already been to an ICBC driver licensing office and requested a Photo BC Services Card, no further action is required.

Information

  • Important: Keep your reference number - write it down, or print this page for your records
  • Allow 21 business days for your application to be processed.
  • After your application is processed, Health Insurance BC will mail a letter to confirm your Medical Services Plan enrolment status. You may have a wait period.
  • When you get your BC Services Card, register for the Fair Pharmacare plan. It can help with the cost of prescription drugs and some medical supplies. Registration is free.
  • Keep your personal information up to date. Change your name, address or gender.
  • Living outside the province for more than six months could cause your Medical Services Plan enrolment to be cancelled. Learn more about eligibility for Medical Services Plan.
  • If you have questions, contact Health Insurance BC.
